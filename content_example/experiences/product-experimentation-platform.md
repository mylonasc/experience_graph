## Context

Product teams were running tests with inconsistent guardrail metrics and ad hoc analysis notebooks. This created slow review cycles and avoidable interpretation errors.

## Contribution

- Standardized experiment assignment, exposure, conversion, and retention tables.
- Built a metric registry with owners, definitions, and valid aggregation windows.
- Added templates for sequential monitoring, heterogeneous effects, and post-test decision memos.
- Coached product managers on interpreting uncertainty and practical significance.

## Outcome

The platform reduced duplicated analysis and made experiment decisions easier to audit after launch.
